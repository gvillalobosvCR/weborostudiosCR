About Us

At Orostudios CR, we are passionate about capturing the moments that make adventure experiences in Costa Rica unique.

We are a Costa Rican company with more than 20 years of experience providing professional photography services in the tourism sector, especially in canopy tours and adventure activities.

Over this time, we have worked with more than 18 canopy parks across the country, developing an operating model that allows businesses to offer their visitors high-quality memories without worrying about logistics, equipment, or staffing.

We currently operate with our own offices and dedicated staff in the main parks we collaborate with, ensuring consistent, excellent service. Our focus is to be strategic partners for parks: we help turn photography into an additional revenue channel, strengthen brand image, and generate professional content that can be used on social networks and marketing campaigns.

How We Work

Orostudios CR’s operations are designed to integrate efficiently and professionally with the park

Our photographic process is designed to guarantee quality, efficiency, and unforgettable memories. We start by accompanying you in your adventure to capture every emotion from the best angle. Then we select and edit your photos with professional detail. Finally, we deliver images ready to relive and share your experience.

01

Setup & Preparation

We carry out an initial refresh of the assigned space with our brand. We install our equipment safely without affecting park operations. We select and train personnel for the site’s specific needs.

02

Daily Operations

Our team covers all park operating hours. We coordinate with guides and park staff to avoid interfering with safety protocols. We capture photos and videos of each customer at strategic points of the tour.

03

Customer Service & Delivery

We serve visitors at the point of sale inside the park. We offer purchasing options with different payment methods. We deliver material via email within a maximum of 24 hours.

04

Monitoring & Quality Control

We perform periodic internal audits to ensure service standards. We carry out preventive maintenance on equipment and update software when necessary.

05

Content Updates

Every six months we carry out special shoots (including drones) to keep the park’s promotional images updated.
